Hero from Final Fantasy VII - Cloud Strife

Cloud Strife begins his journey with a broken sense of identity after a traumatic event involving Shinra and the SOLDIER program leaves his memories confused and incomplete. Because of this, he doesn't truly remember who he is and believes a version of his past that isn't accurate. He meets the resistance group AVALANCHE when they hire him as a mercenary, and through his time with them he slowly uncovers the truth about himself. His main challenge is not only facing Shinra and Sephiroth, but also confronting his own distorted memories and discovering his real identity.
